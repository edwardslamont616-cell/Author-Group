About This Property

Beautifully restored Victorian gem in the heart of Heritage Hill historic district. This stunning home combines original architectural details with thoughtful modern updates. Original hardwood floors gleam throughout, intricate woodwork showcases period craftsmanship, and restored stained glass windows create enchanting light patterns. The updated kitchen blends modern convenience with period charm, featuring vintage-inspired cabinetry and period-appropriate finishes. Restored bathrooms maintain historical character while providing contemporary comfort. A magnificent wrap-around porch offers the perfect space for summer evenings and community gatherings. Located on a tree-lined street just steps from downtown Grand Rapids, Frederik Meijer Gardens, and some of the city's best restaurants and shops. This is a rare opportunity to own a carefully preserved piece of Grand Rapids history.

Neighborhood: Heritage Hill

Heritage Hill is one of Michigan's most prestigious historic neighborhoods, known for its tree-lined streets, Victorian and early 20th-century architecture, and vibrant community. Residents enjoy close proximity to downtown Grand Rapids, Frederik Meijer Gardens, East Hills Schools, and an abundance of dining and shopping options. The neighborhood is perfect for families, professionals, and history enthusiasts who appreciate architectural heritage and community character.
